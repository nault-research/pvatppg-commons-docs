# What is metadata?

"<b>Metadata</b> is structured information that describes, explains, locates, or otherwise makes it easier to retrieve, use, or manage an information resource. Metadata is often called data about data or information about information. It ensures that the context for how your data was created, analyzed and stored, is clear, detailed and therefore, reproducible."

_- National Information Standards Organization, 2004_
