## Exploration <img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/uc-cdis/data-portal/refs/heads/master/src/img/icons/exploration.svg" width="20">

!!! example "Example: Finding and download all mean arterial pressure data for Dahl SS rats"
    **Scenario**  
    “I want to examine accross all cohorts for the PPG whether the blood pressure for rats was similar.”

    **Action**  

    1. Go to the [PVAT PPG Commons](https://data.pvatppgmsu.com).
    2. Login with your commons credentials (MSU credentials are accepted)
    3. Choose the exploration tab.
    4. Go to the _Files_ tab of the right hand bar.
    3. Set **Data Type = Blood Pressure**.
    4. Click on the blue GUID (Globally Unique Identifier).

    **Outcome**  
    “Great, now you have the blood pressure datasets for all cohorts. You can process them in excel, or use more advanced features to map sample to treatment, diet, or other.”
